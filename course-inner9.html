Course Overview

Our course for class 9 at our school is designed to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students for the challenges of high school and beyond. It is structured to enhance the academic, social, and emotional growth of students, with a focus on building strong foundations in key subjects such as mathematics, science, social studies, language, and literature. Along with academic skills, students are also encouraged to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills through various extracurricular activities and projects. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong academic foundation, enabling them to make informed decisions about their future education and career choices

What will the child learn

Learn concepts such as motion, energy, perform experiments and conduct investigations

Learn about geometry, algebra, trigonometry, and other advanced mathematical concepts

Study the history, geography, and civics of India and the world

Languages such as English, Hindi, and a third language of their choice

Explore various art forms such as music, dance, drama, and visual arts

Engage in sports and physical activities that promote fitness and well-being
