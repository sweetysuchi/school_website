Course Overview

Our Class 10 curriculum is designed to provide students with a comprehensive and well-rounded education that prepares them for their future academic and career goals. Our students will engage in challenging coursework that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and independent learning. The curriculum emphasizes academic excellence, character development, and the acquisition of essential life skills. We strive to create a positive and nurturing learning environment that promotes intellectual curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning

What will the child learn

Learn concepts of algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and probability, among others

Study topics such as physics, chemistry, and biology, and scientific principles

Learn about history, civics, geography, and economics, an understanding of the world

Develop their language skills in English and one or more regional languages

Develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills

Engage in sports and physical activities that promote fitness and well-being
