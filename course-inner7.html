Course Overview

Our Class 7 curriculum is designed to provide a smooth transition from the foundational knowledge gained in earlier classes to more advanced concepts. Students will engage in a diverse range of subjects, including English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and more. They will also develop skills in critical thinking, problem-solving, and independent learning. The course will focus on developing well-rounded students who are ready to take on the challenges of higher education.

What will the child learn

Building vocabulary, comprehension, creative writing, and communication skills

Concepts such as decimals, fractions, geometry, algebra, and data analysis

Deeper understanding of biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science

History, civics, geography, and economics

Proficiency in reading, writing, and speaking

Physical activity, sports, and games for physical and mental development

Art and Music: developing creativity, aesthetic sense, and artistic skills
