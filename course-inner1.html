Course Overview

The course curriculum for Class 1 is designed to provide a strong foundation in academics and holistic development. Students will learn basic language, mathematical and scientific concepts, social skills, and ethical values. The curriculum emphasizes experiential learning, creative thinking, and practical application of knowledge. The focus is on nurturing curiosity, imagination, and a lifelong love for learning.

What will the child learn

Basic math skills including addition, subtraction, and simple multiplication

Reading and writing skills, including learning sight words and building basic vocabulary

Science topics such as the solar system, plants, and animals

Social studies topics such as community helpers, family, and traditions

Art and music appreciation and basic skills
