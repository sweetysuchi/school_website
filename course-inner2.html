Course Overview

Our Class 2 curriculum is designed to build on the foundational knowledge and skills learned in Class 1, while encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration. Our students will explore a wide range of subjects, including language arts, mathematics, science, social studies, and more. Our experienced teachers use a variety of instructional techniques, such as hands-on activities, projects, and technology-based learning, to keep our students engaged and motivated. By the end of the year, our Class 2 students will have developed a strong academic foundation and a love of learning that will serve them well in the years to come.

What will the child learn

English: Reading, writing, comprehension skills, basic grammar and vocabulary building

Mathematics: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division and basic geometry

Science: Basic concepts related to plants, animals, environment, and weather

Social study: history, geography, and civic studies, understanding communities and cultures

Art and Crafts: Drawing, coloring, and basic crafting skills
