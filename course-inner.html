Course Overview

The Nursery program at our school is designed to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment that supports young learners in their cognitive, social, and emotional development. Our curriculum focuses on providing a strong foundation in basic skills such as language, math, and socialization. We believe that learning through play is an effective way for young children to develop their cognitive and motor skills, so we incorporate a variety of activities, such as games, storytelling, music, and art, into our lessons.


Our experienced and dedicated teachers provide a safe and caring environment that encourages exploration, creativity, and curiosity. We strive to create a positive learning experience that fosters a love of learning in our young students and prepares them for success in their academic journey.

What will the child learn

Language & communication skills, including vocabulary, listening comprehension & speaking

Pre-reading and pre-writing skills and basic writing strokes

Basic math concepts, such as counting, number recognition simple addition & subtraction

Socialization skills, including sharing, taking turns, & cooperating with others

Gross motor skillsand fine motor skills such as running, climbing, drawing & pasting
